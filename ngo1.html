Our Mission : Graphhene Foundation is an educational NGO that has a vision, a dream to educate every unprivileged child in our country. It's a long, long journey and we've our seat belts on. We started on the streets and moved on to villages and cities. And since then we have never looked back. Running an educational ngo for unprivileged children has become a way of life for us. In the modern world education is necessary to survive. The digital revolution has taken over the world. Everything has gone online. It becomes further necessary to provide education to the future of this country.Graphene foundation is about a permanent change. We focus on not just teaching books but more importantly life values. We teach them an intelligent and honest way of living. We encourage independent thinking and developing skills that are unique to each child. We believe in this generation. We believe in a better future.
