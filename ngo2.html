Our Mission : CRY (Child Rights and You) is a non-profit, non-governmental organization (NGO) focused on ensuring children's rights and well-being in India. Registered under the Indian Trusts Act and Foreign Contribution Regulation Act, CRY is a developmental NGO (DNGO) accredited by GuideStar India, Credibility Alliance, and Indian Institute of Corporate Affairs. As a child-focused organization, CRY works on child education, health, protection, livelihoods, and disaster response, benefiting over 3 million children across 19 states. CRY is a member of UNICEF, Child Rights International Network, and Asian Pacific Resource and Research Centre for Women, and is eligible for tax exemption under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act.
