Our Mission : Chirag Foundation's mission is to empower rural communities through holistic development, focusing on education, livelihoods, and community development. They aim to bridge the rural-urban divide by providing access to quality education, skill development, and economic opportunities, enabling individuals to become self-sufficient and contribute to their community's growth. By working with marginalized groups, especially women and children, Chirag Foundation strives to create a sustainable, equitable, and prosperous society where individuals can thrive and reach their full potential.
