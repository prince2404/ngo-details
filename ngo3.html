Our Mission : The Akshaya Patra Foundation is a non-profit NGO that aims to provide nutritious and hygienic meals to school-going children, particularly in rural and disadvantaged areas. Its mission is "No child in India shall be deprived of education because of hunger," focusing on addressing hunger and malnutrition as major obstacles to education. By providing mid-day meals, Akshaya Patra seeks to improve attendance, enrollment, and retention rates in schools, while also enhancing the health and well-being of beneficiaries. Since its inception in 2000, the organization has grown to become one of the largest NGO-run mid-day meal programs globally, serving over 1.8 million children daily across 12 states in India.
